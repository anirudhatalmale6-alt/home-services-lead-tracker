Rental Home Painting FAQs

Rental home painting is a cost-effective painting solution designed for homes that are being vacated or prepared for new tenants. It focuses on neat finish, wall touch-ups, and fresh look without expensive decorative work.

Basic rental home painting generally includes: Single-coat or double-coat wall painting, minor wall patchwork, standard colour application, and clean and uniform finish suitable for rental use.

Premium rental painting offers better finish and durability compared to basic painting. It includes improved surface preparation and higher-quality paint for a more refined appearance.

Our team will inspect your home and suggest basic or premium painting based on wall condition, age of property, and your rental requirements.
