Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't you show fixed prices?

Every post construction or commercial cleaning project is unique. Pricing depends on area size (sq.ft), property type, dust/debris level, surface materials (marble, tiles, wood), and manpower required. A free site inspection allows us to assess your exact needs and provide an accurate, transparent quote.

What equipment do you use for post construction cleaning?

We use industrial-grade equipment including high-powered vacuum machines for dust extraction, single disc scrubbers for floor cleaning, high-pressure jet cleaners for tough stains, and steam cleaners for sanitization. All combined with eco-friendly chemicals that are safe for your surfaces.

How long does post construction cleaning take?

Timeline depends on the area size and scope of work. A typical 2-3 BHK apartment takes 1-2 days, while larger commercial projects may take several days. We'll provide a detailed timeline estimate during the site inspection and quote stage.

Do you provide cleaning for builder handovers?

Yes, we work with builders, contractors, and real estate developers for project handover cleaning. We can handle multiple units in apartment complexes with volume discounts available. Contact us to discuss your project requirements.

Is site inspection really free?

Yes, absolutely free with no obligations. Our team will visit your site, assess the scope of work, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed quote. You're free to compare and decide - there's no pressure or hidden charges.
