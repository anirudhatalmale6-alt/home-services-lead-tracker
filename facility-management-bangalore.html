Frequently Asked Questions

What types of facilities do you manage?

We manage housekeeping, security and pantry services for manufacturing units, large residential complexes, tech parks, corporate offices, hospitals, shopping centers and educational institutions across Bangalore.

Do you provide security guards on annual contracts?

Yes, all our security services operate on annual maintenance plans. This ensures uninterrupted protection with trained, experienced guards equipped with walkie-talkies, proper uniforms and emergency response training.

What equipment do your security guards carry?

Our security guards are equipped with walkie-talkies for instant communication, proper uniforms with ID badges, torches for night patrol, and undergo complete professional training including emergency response and fire safety protocols.

Yes, currently our facility management services are available only in Bangalore. We cover all major areas including Whitefield, Electronic City, Koramangala, Marathahalli, Peenya, Hebbal and surrounding zones.
