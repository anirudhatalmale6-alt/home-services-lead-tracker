Sofa & Carpet Shampooing

Over time, sofas and carpets absorb dust, allergens, pet dander, food particles, and odours that regular vacuuming cannot remove. Stain-33's professional sofa and carpet shampooing service in Bangalore uses advanced extraction technology to deep-clean upholstery and carpets, restoring them to their original freshness. Our process involves pre-treatment of stains, application of premium foam shampoo, mechanical agitation to loosen embedded dirt, and hot water extraction to pull out all contaminants. The result is visibly cleaner, softer, and fresher-smelling fabrics. We handle all types of upholstery materials including fabric, microfibre, leather, and velvet, adjusting our cleaning technique and products accordingly. For carpets, we offer both on-site shampooing and pickup-and-delivery deep cleaning for larger or heavily soiled pieces. Our technicians are trained to identify fabric types and choose the appropriate cleaning method to prevent damage or colour bleeding. After shampooing, we apply a fabric protector that helps repel future stains and extends the life of your upholstery. Experience the comfort of truly clean furniture with Stain-33's expert shampooing services.
