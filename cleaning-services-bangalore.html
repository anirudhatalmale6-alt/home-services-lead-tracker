Sofa & Carpet Shampooing

Over time, sofas and carpets absorb dust, allergens, food particles, and odours that regular vacuuming cannot remove. Stain-33's sofa and carpet shampooing service in Bangalore uses advanced extraction technology to deep-clean upholstery and restore original freshness. Our process involves pre-treatment of stains, premium foam shampoo application, mechanical agitation to loosen embedded dirt, and hot water extraction. We handle all upholstery materials including fabric, microfibre, leather, and velvet, adjusting our technique accordingly. After shampooing, we apply a fabric protector that helps repel future stains and extends upholstery life.
