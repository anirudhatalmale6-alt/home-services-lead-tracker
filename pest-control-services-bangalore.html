Our Pest Control Services

Pest Control Services in Bangalore

Stain-33 delivers comprehensive pest control services across Bangalore, protecting your home and workplace from common pests. Our certified technicians use government-approved, premium imported chemicals and advanced application methods for maximum effectiveness. Whether you are dealing with cockroaches in your kitchen, ants in your pantry, or spiders in corners, our general pest control treatment covers it all. We begin with a thorough property inspection to identify pest entry points, breeding areas, and infestation severity, then deploy targeted treatments including residual sprays, gel baits, and barrier applications that continue working long after our visit.

Termite Control & Treatment

Termites are among the most destructive pests, capable of causing extensive structural damage if left untreated. Stain-33 offers both pre-construction and post-construction termite control solutions in Bangalore, designed to eliminate existing colonies and prevent future infestations. Our pre-construction treatment creates a chemical barrier in the soil before foundation laying, providing up to five years of warranty protection. For existing buildings, we use the proven drill-fill-seal method, injecting termiticide into walls, floors, and wooden structures. Our advanced baiting systems offer a non-invasive alternative, using strategically placed bait stations that attract and eliminate entire colonies over time.

Cockroach & Bed Bug Treatment

Cockroaches and bed bugs are among the most persistent household pests in Bangalore. Stain-33's specialised treatments target these resilient insects with a multi-pronged approach for lasting results. Our cockroach gel treatment uses odourless, long-lasting gel bait applied in cracks, crevices, and behind appliances. The gel eliminates even hidden populations through a cascading colony effect, lasting three to six months with no need to vacate your home. For bed bugs, we combine chemical spray with steam treatment to target all life stages including eggs, nymphs, and adults across mattresses, bed frames, and furniture. Treatments are delivered as a two-visit package for complete elimination.

Mosquito & Rodent Control

Mosquitoes and rodents pose significant health risks, carrying diseases such as dengue, malaria, and leptospirosis. Stain-33 provides targeted mosquito and rodent control services across Bangalore to safeguard your family's health. Our mosquito control program uses residual spray treatments applied to indoor and outdoor areas, creating a protective barrier effective for thirty days or more. We treat walls, curtains, under furniture, gardens, and stagnant water areas. Our rodent control services employ tamper-proof bait stations, mechanical traps, and entry-point sealing to eliminate rats and mice. Our technicians identify rodent pathways and nesting sites to deploy the most effective control measures for lasting protection.
